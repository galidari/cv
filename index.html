Dana Huff

CSW, LCADC

Dana Huff is a licensed clinician in the field of addiction recovery. She graduated Summa Cum Laude from Eastern Kentucky University with a bachelor of social work. She graduated with honors from the social work program at the University of Kentucky where she received her masters of social work degree.

She has nearly ten years of experience in the addiction care field. During her career she has also served as a director of case management as well as created a family care program.
